Questions you may ask

What does Motion of Naimur specialize in?
▼
Motion of Naimur is a performance-focused video editing agency specializing in UGC ads, short-form vertical videos, paid social creatives, and VSL-style content for Meta, TikTok, and digital advertising platforms.
What type of UGC content do you edit?
▼
We edit problem-solution ads, testimonial UGC, before-after ads, creator-led vertical videos, product demos, hook-based short ads, and conversion-optimized creatives designed for paid campaigns.
Do you work with agencies or direct brands?
▼
We collaborate with marketing agencies, media buyers, and direct-to-consumer brands. Our workflow is structured to fit seamlessly into agency pipelines and performance marketing systems.
How do you ensure ad performance and consistency?
▼
Every project follows a clear structure: hook strategy, pacing, messaging hierarchy, visual retention techniques, and platform-specific optimization—ensuring consistency across multiple ad variations.
Why choose an agency-style editor over freelancers?
▼
You get system-driven editing, scalable delivery, fast turnaround, and performance awareness. Our process is built for volume, testing, and long-term collaboration— not one-off edits.
What does your editing workflow look like?
▼
Brief & references → hook strategy → raw footage review → structured edit → motion & captions → platform optimization → revisions → final delivery. Clean, fast, and agency-ready.
